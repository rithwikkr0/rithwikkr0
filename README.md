### 🧠 About Me

I'm an AI/ML student (IITG AIML Trimester program) with a hands-on builder's instinct — I don't just study deep learning and computer vision, I wire up the hardware to put it to work. My projects sit at the intersection of **AI**, **embedded systems**, and **security-aware design**: ESP32 robots that talk back, browser extensions that automate AI video pipelines, and apps built to solve problems I've actually run into.

- 🔬 Currently deepening my skills in **Deep Learning, Computer Vision, and NLP**

- 🎯 Finalist, **OpenAI Buildathon** · Participant, multiple **Hack2Skills** national hackathons
- 📚 Always learning in public — Kaggle, LeetCode, HackerRank, Coursera
